Quizhub

A custom quizzer app/api that aims to be fun and help with self education. Users can contribute to a growing list of community generated quizzes by creating their own private or public quizzes, and questions. There is also a variety of trivia categories and challenges to choose from if you just want to test your knowledge.

App Specs

  • *Framework: Rails
  • *Javascript: AJAX
  • *Database: Postgresql
  • *Authentication: Omniauth
  • *Deployment: Heroku

DC Pet Connection

Is a social networking application for pet owners in the DC area, designed to help users make connections in their selected neighborhood. Once logged in, you can create your own custom profile for your pets, and even interact with neighbors through writing and tagging posts.

App Specs

  • *Framework: Sinatra
  • *Database: Postgresql
  • *Database: ActiveRecord
  • *Authentication: Bcrypt
  • *Deployment: Heroku
